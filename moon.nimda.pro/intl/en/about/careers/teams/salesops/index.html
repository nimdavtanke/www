Sales Operations

Set the direction for our business and make sure it runs smoothly.

Our Sales Operations teams think strategically and globally, setting the direction for our business and then making sure we run smoothly. Our teams have developed and implemented next-generation advertising products, defined strategies to optimize operations and established initiatives in emerging markets. And it doesn’t end with implementation--we measure our impact on revenue, cost and operational efficiency. What can we say? We’re a company who loves data and a team who loves contributing to the bottom line.
