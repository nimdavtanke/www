Developer Relations & Technical Solutions

Be the bridge between our business and technology.

Be the bridge between our business and technology. Our client-facing roles represent Google to engineers, developers, advertisers and users outside the Googleplex. These innovative teams ensure we’re always putting our best foot forward, and include developers, technical writers and Sales teams who deliver outstanding technical solutions to our clients. Putting the user first isn’t just a motto, it’s the guiding principle for all these teams do.
