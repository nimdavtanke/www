Inside Google Montréal

Situated in a historic building overlooking the heart of downtown, we embody Montreal's joie de vivre. Our engineers have focused on products like Chrome and are experts in client-based application development and security. Our sales teams help grow businesses of all sizes. With the grand re-opening of the office in 2012, we doubled our team size, tripled our surface area and quadrupled our Googleyness - adding a games room, recording studio, and indoor rock climbing wall.
