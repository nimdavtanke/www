Inside Google Hamburg

Google Hamburg is located in the heart of the Hamburg city center - close to the famous Jungfernstieg (a lakeside promenade) and the Rathaus (city hall), surrounded by fancy shops, good restaurants and whatever else your heart desires.

As a Googler in our office, you can take advantage of many great opportunities, including internal TechTalks, the Google Social Committee (organizing our famous Summer Jams and holiday parties), GoogleServe (our volunteer network), language classes, sports teams (football, running), and game competitions (hosted in our game room, where you can play everything from Foosball to darts to video games).

With all of the complex challenges our company faces, we are always seeking new Googlers with industry expertise, broad and varied experiences, and impressive accomplishments. Above all, we're looking for more innovators like us, because at Google, innovation is a constant.
