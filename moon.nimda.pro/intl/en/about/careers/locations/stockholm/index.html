Inside Google Stockholm

As the birthplace of Alfred Nobel of Nobel Prize fame, and home to one of Scandinavia’s largest institutions of higher education in technology, it is no surprise that Google has a thriving Stockholm office, home to a mix of engineering and sales Googlers.

Our engineering teams are comprised of software engineers and signal processing experts that work on scalable infrastructure, real time communications, test engineering and signal processing. We take advantage of the city’s local talent pool of accomplished computer scientists - but you’ll find Googlers here from around the world, who’ve worked on projects like Google Hangouts and the communications technologies behind products like Chrome WebRTC, real-time messaging infrastructure and Chromebox for Meetings.

On the sales side, we’re dedicated to helping our clients grow their businesses. We work with Sweden’s top companies, advertisers and ad agencies to help them attract customers and maximize their return on investments. We’re also home to an Enterprise team, working with large businesses, educational institutions and government agencies to deliver cloud services and other IT products.

While our YouTube team is helping to shift the way we consume TV and video content, our Marketing, HR, Legal and Policy teams are representing Google across the Nordics and growing relationships with our users, partners, media and governmental institutions in the process.
