Inside Google Atlanta

Google Atlanta is located in the heart of midtown, surrounded by culturally enriching venues like theaters and art centers as well as educational powerhouses such as Georgia Tech and Emory University.

As one of the main Google offices in the southern United States, you'll experience friendly southern hospitality from each Googler right when you walk in the doors. We actively take part in the office culture and activities, such as tech talks, coffee talk in the Terminus Cafe, and our bi-weekly celebration at TGIF. And we love getting out of the office to help others and the community with GoogleServe events, like taking part in local Pride parades and giving back to schools and non-profits in our community. We also team up with the Goolgers at our nearby Douglas County data center, located just 15 miles away. Even as our workforce grows, when you visit, you instantly see that we strive to know everyone's name and share a strong bond of camaraderie.
