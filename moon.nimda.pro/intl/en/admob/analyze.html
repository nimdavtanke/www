Understand your users

AdMob has the tools you need to understand your users and act on those insights. Enjoy all the benefits of Google Analytics directly within AdMob, and use the knowledge gained to segment your audiences to track their lifetime value.

Google Analytics fully integrated into AdMob

Google Analytics is free to use and you can access all the metrics and dimensions that truly matter for your app, directly in the AdMob interface. Get a complete view of your apps in real time, with insights into user acquisition, engagement, and monetization—all in one place. Screen views, sessions, app version, and screen size are just a few of the app-specific concepts you’ll find, so you can make informed decisions.

Segment audiences

Segment your audiences to gain insights into how people use your app differently. For example, segment by age, location, and amount spent to see which group of users is driving the most in-app purchases (virtual goods and e-commerce). Sequence-based segments offer deeper insights and can help uncover new opportunities to increase revenue.

Build custom strategies

Audience Builder is a powerful tool that enables you to create lists of audiences based on how they use your app. With this data you can create customized experiences for your users, and grow your app revenue. For example, you could create an Audience List that defines those users who’ve been using your app for more than 30 days but haven’t made a purchase. Why not target these users with an AdMob house ads campaign promoting your other apps, and convert them into paying users? This same Audience List is also available in AdWords, so you can run a paid remarketing campaign to re-engage these users while they’re in other apps. There are countless other ways to use Audience Builder, which you can start using from the Analyze tab of your AdMob account.

Keep users engaged

Build a successful apps business by understanding the actions users are taking in your Android or iOS apps in real time. Behavioral reports and flow visualization show you the exact screens where users take action, pause, or disappear. Pinpoint issues quickly so you can improve your app and keep users engaged.

Optimize Google Play installs

The Google Play Flow Referral report in Analytics highlights which campaigns are driving the most views, installs, and new users in Google Play. Use the insights to optimize app marketing campaigns, your Play app description page and installs.
